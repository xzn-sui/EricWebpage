STAR Interview Method

The STAR method is a structured way to answer behavioral interview questions by explaining the Situation, Task, Action, and Result.

Component Description
Situation Describe the context and background of the experience.
Task Explain the responsibility or challenge you needed to address.
Action Detail the specific steps you took to handle the situation.
Result Share the outcome and what you learned.
